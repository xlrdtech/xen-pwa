Why XP wins

Beeper gives you cross-network chat. Spark gives you email. HubSpot gives you a CRM. None of them gives you all three voice-first, push-realtime, with one identity wearing the right mask per thread. XP does.

01

Voice-first by design

STT, TTS, barge-in baked in. Every thread reads itself, every reply is dictated. Hands-free OS.

02

Push-realtime

SSE on every new arrival. New email, new SMS, new WhatsApp ping — the screen updates inside a second. No refresh.

03

Persona routing

Same OS, multiple identities. Different signatures per thread without the cross-talk risk that kills multi-account apps.

04

Universal connector

Plug-and-play with any AI agent: Claude, GPT, Gemini, Grok. URL-as-credential, no auth headers, just works.
